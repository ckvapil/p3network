WHAT IS P3?
The Public Private Partnership Network also known as P3 Network is facilitated by volunteers of Commit! and is comprised of representatives from 12 different Dallas area private schools and all 17 elementary schools (serving approximately 10,750 students) within the Thomas Jefferson and WT White Feeder Patterns of DISD. Each participant is also a part of an individual partnership representing at least one public and one private institution.

P3 OBJECTIVE
To create collaborative partnerships that target advancing one of the eleven key indicators, 3rd Grade Reading Scores, as identified by the Commit! Advisory Council. Each partnership is working on individual programming to fit the needs of individual elementary schools.

P3 PROGRAMMING
Below are some examples of our partnership programming in schools:
  • One-on-one or small group reading tutors
  • Installation of College Going Culture Environment Materials
  • Creation of tutor training materials
  • Organization of book drives to supplement campus or classroom inventories
  • Leveling current book inventories
  • Creating Reading Buddy programs across similar grades on each campus
  • Inviting public school parent community to share in speaker programs on private school campuses
